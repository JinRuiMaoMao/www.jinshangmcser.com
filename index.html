什么是MTR?

Minecraft Transit Railway（简称 MTR） 是一款致力于在《我的世界》中重现真实轨道交通体验的模组。通过它，玩家不仅可以建造铁路线路，更能实现自动化运行的地铁、城际列车与轻轨系统。列车可根据设定自动进站、发车，并支持显示屏、广播、时刻表、信号系统等多种真实元素，让城市交通在方块世界中焕发生机。

我们的世界

在 金尚市，这一模组被充分运用到极致。我们的服务器以 MTR 为核心，构建了一个庞大、复杂、且不断扩展的轨道交通网络。这里的每一条线路、每一个车站、每一辆列车都由玩家亲手设计、精心打造。

无论是繁忙的市中心地铁、宁静的郊区支线，还是穿越高架的观光线路，玩家都能感受到接近现实都市的出行体验。

• 真实车站设计：还原现实地铁的建筑风格与结构布局。

• 多样列车型号：涵盖市域、城际、机场快线等不同类型。

• 精准时刻系统：自动化运营、定点发车，让地铁如同现实般运行。

• 完善换乘网络：多线互通，轻松换乘，体验高效便捷的出行。

在金尚市，地铁不仅是一种交通方式，更是一种艺术与工程的结合。这里汇聚了热爱建筑、城市规划与交通系统的玩家，共同打造一个充满未来感与秩序之美的虚拟都市轨道世界。

What is MTR?

Minecraft Transit Railway (MTR) is a mod dedicated to creating a realistic railway and metro system inside Minecraft. With this mod, players can design and operate fully functional transit systems — including automated trains, signaling, announcements, real-time displays, and timetables — all working together to bring modern urban transport into the blocky world.

Our World

In JinShang, the MTR mod has been developed to its full potential. Our server is built around this mod, featuring a massive and intricate rail network that continues to grow. Every line, every station, and every train is carefully crafted by our players with creativity and precision.

From bustling metro lines in the downtown area to peaceful suburban routes and scenic elevated railways, players can experience a transport system that feels truly alive.

• Realistic Station Design: Architecture and layouts inspired by real-world metro systems.

• Variety of Train Models: From city metros to intercity and airport express lines.

• Accurate Timetables: Automated operations ensure punctual arrivals and departures.

• Comprehensive Transfer Network: Multiple lines interconnect seamlessly for smooth transfers.

In JinShang, the metro is more than just a means of transportation — it’s a fusion of engineering, creativity, and urban design. Our community brings together players passionate about architecture, city planning, and transportation, building a futuristic and well-ordered virtual metropolis powered by MTR.
